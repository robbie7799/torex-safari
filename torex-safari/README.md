# Torex Safari WordPress Theme

This theme is built to showcase Uganda tourism (Torex Safari). It includes:
- Attractions custom post type (CPT)
- Regions taxonomy to group attractions
- Customizer settings for UTB (Uganda Tourism Board) and URSB (Uganda Registration Services Bureau) links and contact info
- Simple responsive styles and templates

Install:
1. Upload to WordPress: Appearance > Themes > Add New > Upload Theme (choose torex-safari.zip).
2. Activate the theme.
3. Go to Appearance > Customize to set UTB and URSB URLs and contact information.
4. Add Attractions via the new Attractions menu. Assign Regions to each attraction.

Suggested attractions to add (names + recommended region taxonomy):
- Bwindi Impenetrable National Park — Region: Southwestern
- Kidepo Valley National Park — Region: Northern
- Murchison Falls National Park — Region: Northern/West Nile
- Kibale National Park — Region: Western
- Ziwa Rhino Sanctuary — Region: Central
- Fort Portal (Crater Lakes & town) — Region: Western
- Queen Elizabeth National Park — Region: Western
- Semuliki National Park — Region: Western
- Lake Bunyonyi — Region: Southwestern
- Sipi Falls — Region: Eastern
- Mount Elgon National Park — Region: Eastern
- Fort Portal Marathon (event) — Region: Western
